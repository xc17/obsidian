#### $\Sigma F_{X} = 0$
$0 = T_{1}\cos 30째 - T_{2} \cos 60째 = 0$
$0 = T_{1} \frac{1}{2} \sqrt{3} - T_{2} \frac{1}{2} = 0$
$0,52T_{2} = 0,87T_{1}$
$\frac{T_{1}}{T_{2}} = \frac{0,5}{0,87}$

$\implies$

$T_{1} = 0,57T_{2}$
$T_{1} = 0,57 \times 17,31$
$T_{1} = 9,886 N$

#### $\Sigma F_{Y} = 0$

$20 = 0,285T_{2} + 0,87T_{2}$
